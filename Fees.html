Fees and Charges

Text Charges

At our engraving service, we offer text engraving for a flat fee of $3 per vertical line. Whether it's a name, date of service, a special message, or any other text, we can beautifully engrave it onto your chosen material. This fee ensures that your personalized text is expertly crafted with precision and attention to detail.

Image Fees

For image engraving, we charge based on the square inch size of the image. Our rate is $1.10 per square inch, which allows us to capture even the most intricate details of your image and engrave it onto your selected item. This fee covers the setup and engraving process to ensure the highest quality and clarity in reproducing your image.

Vector Creation

When it comes to image manipulation for laser engraving or CNC routing, vectors are essential. Vectors are mathematical representations of shapes, allowing for precise scaling and editing without compromising the image quality. By converting images to vectors, we can ensure that the engravings are accurate, sharp, and maintain their integrity across different sizes and materials. The process of creating vectors involves skilled graphic design work and meticulous attention to detail to optimize the image for engraving.

Retail Items

Please note that our retail items do not include engraving charges. We believe in providing our customers with the freedom to choose and personalize their items according to their preferences and budget. By separating the engraving charges, you have the flexibility to select the level of customization that suits your needs. This approach allows you to have more control over the final cost, giving you the freedom to invest in the engraving options that are most important to you. At our engraving service, we strive to deliver exceptional craftsmanship and customization options while being transparent about our fees and charges. If you have any further questions or would like a detailed quote for your specific engraving needs, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team.
