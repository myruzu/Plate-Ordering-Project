Fiber laser engraving and CNC Engraving
 are two different techniques  that can produce different results on plated metals like bronze and aluminum.

CNC Engraving and The Surface Finish

CNC engraving with a diamond drag can impart a textured or three-dimensional look to the metal surface due to the dragging motion of the tool, resulting in a distinctive aesthetic effect. However, it may not achieve the same level of smoothness and polish as laser engraving. In contrast, fiber laser engraving typically yields a sleek and flawless surface finish, while CNC routing showcases remarkable brilliance.
