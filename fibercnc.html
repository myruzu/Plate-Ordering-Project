Fiber laser engraving and CNC Engraving are two different technologies that produce different results on plated metals like bronze and aluminum.

CNC Engraving and The Surface Finish

CNC engraving with a "diamond drag" style engraving needle point can impart a textured almost three-dimensional look to the metal's surface due to the dragging motion of the tool. CNC routing showcases remarkable brilliance and a unique aesthetic due to the hundreds or thousands of lines carved into the metal's surface. These lines reflect the light to give the illusion of a 3D texture.

Fiber Laser Engraving and The Surface Finish

The engraving itself possesses exceptional clarity and fine detail, thanks to the precision of fiber laser technology. Each stroke and line is meticulously etched, resulting in sharp edges and well-defined shapes.
